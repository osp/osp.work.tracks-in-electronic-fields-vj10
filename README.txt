Tracks in electr(on)ic fields-VJ10
==================================

Book
----

Publication containing texts and images from **Verbindingen/Jonctions 10:
Tracks in electr(on)ic fields**, organised by Constant in 2007. Designed by
Pierre Huyghebaert and Femke Snelting with ConTeXt (+ Gimp, Inkscape and
Scribus for the cover). Photos by Ilan Weiss (thanks!).

Laureate [Prix Fernand
Baudin](http://www.prixfernandbaudinprijs.be/Public/Book.php?ID=3413)
